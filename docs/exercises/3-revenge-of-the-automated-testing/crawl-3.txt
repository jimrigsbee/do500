The Project is crumbling under attacks by the ruthless Sith Lord, Regression. There are failures on both client and server sides. Bugs are everywhere.

In a stunning move, the heroic Jedi developers, Mocha Skywalker and his friend Hans Jest have swept into the Galactic codebase and rescued Code Integrity, leader of the Galactic Development Principles.

As the Separatist army of Sith Developers attempt to flee the besieged project with their valuable hostage, manual testing. Two Jedi Knights lead a desperate mission with an alliance of jenkins build jobs to automate the running of functional tests in the pipeline and demonstrate their commitment to the republic by implementing a new feature using TDD.
