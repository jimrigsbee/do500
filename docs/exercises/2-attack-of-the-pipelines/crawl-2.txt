There is unrest in the Project Senate. Several thousand stakeholders have declared their intentions to run the code in their own environments at their own version.

This separatist movement, under the leadership of the mysterious aCount Managers, have made it difficult for the limited number of Jedi Developers to maintain peace and order in the galaxy.

Senator Jenkins, the former Queen of Hudson, is returning to the Project Senate to implement a pipeline to production by creating an ARMY OF BUILD JOBS FOR THE RESIDENCY to assist the overwhelmed Jedi developers....
