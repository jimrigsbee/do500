Evading the dreaded Imperial Starfleet, a group of security scanners led by Zap Scanwalker has established a new secret base on the remote ice world of Docker.

The genius Jedi Scanwalker, obsessed with finding a solution to the Pipeline problem, has dispatched thousands of linters and code coverage reporters into the far reaches of Cloud City in hopes to improve builds,thus enhancing their pipeline quality...
